Tirley Garth

Address

Tirley Garth
Mallows Way
Willington
Nr. Tarporey
Cheshire & Wirral
CW6 0RQ

Telephone

01829 732301

Location

 2½ miles north of Tarporley, just north of Utkinton on road to Kelsall.

Opening Times

1pm–5pm; Sundays; 12 May - 2 June (except 26 May). Please call to confirm opening dates and times.

Admission

Adults £5; Children free. Please call to confirm admission prices.

Facilities

  

Features

  • Alpine
  • Bluebells
  • Herbaceous plants
  • Camellias
  • Fine conifers
  • Daffodils
  • Fruit of special interest
  • Rhododendrons
  • Rock garden

Owner

Private Owner

Comment

Tirley Garth (originally Tirley Court) is a famous example of Thomas Mawson's work: wonderful terraces, paths, retaining walks and garden buildings. It is this structure which underpins the planting, including the rhododendrons and azaleas in the woodland below. Restoration, replanting and improvement have continued in recent years.
