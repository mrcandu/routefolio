Worth a Visit

Exemplary town-centre bar and coffee bar in well run civilised old hotel, friendly and easy-going, with well kept ales and enjoyable good value food all day from breakfast on, separate brasserie/grill room with adjoining champagne bar; heated tables out in front, river across busy main road, bedrooms
